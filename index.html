ABOUT THE MASQUERADE.

The Masquerade festival is an annual event celebrated by the people of Ghana It's main attraction is dozens of groups of people or teams dressing in wild outfits who display their skills and arts via dance. Masquerade in Africa is a rich and diverse cultural tradition that has played a significant role in African religious, social, and political life for centuries.
